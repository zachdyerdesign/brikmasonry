Brick by Brik Masonry

We are family owned and operated and do historical building brick and/or stone color matching and repair. We are bonded, licensed and insured.

At Brik Masonry we specialize in the installation of Stone, Brick and Block masonry providing these service for jobs ranging from larger commercial buildings to multi-family complexes and new construction.

The business began in 2001 in Southwest Missouri and has since then expanded to surrounding states including Oklahoma, Arkansas, Texas, Iowa, Kansas, N. Dakota, Indiana and Illinois.

The best Brik Masonry in town

It has always been our goal to provide the highest quality of work at competitive prices. Brik Masonry works closely with general contractors, architects, and suppliers to guarantee projects are finished on schedule and within budget.

Our commitment to quality workmanship, integrity, workplace safety and superior customer service enables us to meet the demands of our clients.
