About {{ site.title }}

Brik Masonry (Brick Mountain Holding LLC) was established in 1992 by founder and manager Brik Huntington. Since that year Brik has striven to build a reputation of being honest and hard working providing a service to the community that is unmatched in the masonry industry.

At Brik Masonry we specialize in the installation of Stone, Brick and Block masonry providing these service for jobs ranging from larger commercial buildings to multi-family complexes and new construction.
